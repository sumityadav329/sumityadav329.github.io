Vectors: Interview Q&A Style

Interviewer: Toh, aap AI mein interested hain. Chalo basic se start karte hain. Math mein aap vectors jaante hain. AI mein vectors ka kya role hai, simple terms mein?

Bilkul. AI mein, vector ek ordered list of numbers hota hai jo kisi cheez ko represent karta hai - jaise ek word, ek image ka patch, ya kisi user ki preferences. Har number uss cheez ka ek specific feature ya dimension represent karta hai.

Think of it like describing a fruit with numbers:

  • Redness (0-10): 9
  • Sweetness (0-10): 7
  • Size (0-10): 5

Toh is Apple ka vector ho sakta hai [9, 7, 5]. AI algorithms in numerical representations par kaam karte hain.

Interviewer: Samajh gaya. Toh yeh data ko numerically represent karne ka tareeka hai. Yeh important kyun hai?

Kyunki computers fundamentally numbers pe operate karte hain. Real-world data (jaise text, images, sound) ko directly process nahi kar sakte. Vectors (aur matrices) use karke hum is data ko ek aise format mein convert karte hain jise algorithms samajh sakein aur process kar sakein.

In vectors ko "embeddings" bhi kaha jaata hai - hum complex information ko ek numerical space mein 'embed' kar rahe hain.
